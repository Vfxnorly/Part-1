ABOUT US

Love From Above was born out of a simple but powerful realization: that true change begins when we look beyond ourselves to serve those in need. Founded on the principles of [Faith/Compassion/Community], we recognized that many in our society were falling through the cracks.What started as a small group of dedicated volunteers has grown into a registered NPO committed to being the hands and feet of hope. We believe that every act of kindness, no matter how small, is a reflection of a greater love.


The Heart Behind Love From Above

At Love From Above, we believe no one should have to navigate life is hardest moments alone. We are a group of neighbors and advocates driven by a simple idea: That everyone deserves to be treated with dignity and given a real shot at a better future. We do not just drop off supplies and leave. We are here to walk alongside people whether that is by providing a warm meal, helping a student find a mentor, or bridging the digital gap so a family can thrive in today is world. Our goal isn't just to help people get by; it is to help them get ahead and stay there. We aren't just an organization, we are a community in action. And we would love for you to be a part of it.
